My Heart On Paper

Dear Babdiiiiii,

As I sit down to write this, my heart is overflowing with so many emotions. Looking back to May 2021, I had no idea that the friend I was making would eventually become the absolute center of my universe. Being in a long-distance relationship isn't easy. It tests patience, strength, and trust, but with you, every single mile between us is completely worth it.

We've shared countless beautiful days, laughed until our stomachs hurt, and cried when the distance felt too heavy. We’ve had our fair share of fights and misunderstandings, but what makes us beautiful is our forgiveness and the way we always find our way back to each other, stronger than before. Your constant support has been my anchor.

Babdiiiiii, I need to take a moment to deeply and sincerely apologize. I know I messed up terribly by forgetting our first anniversary and your birthday this year. There are no excuses for it. It breaks my heart knowing that I might have made you feel unappreciated on days that are so profoundly important. You are the most precious person in my life, and I am genuinely so sorry for hurting you. This website, these words, are my small way of showing you that I care, that I remember everything else, and that I deeply value you.

I promise to be a better partner. I promise to be more attentive, to celebrate you the way you deserve to be celebrated, and to never let you doubt your place in my heart. You are my home, my comfort, and my greatest blessing.

Happy 2nd Anniversary, my love. Let's keep writing this beautiful story.

With all my love,
Pranay
